EVERY ARTICLE GETS A SECOND LIFE

The distribution engine takes existing content — Substack articles, podcast transcripts — and generates branded carousels in the Fighting for a Free Future design system. Recognisable at thumbnail size. Shareable without explanation.

Each carousel is extracted from the article's argument arc: setup, thesis, evidence, kicker. The brand builds in feeds before anyone ever visits the website.
